About Uncle Warren

Warren Buffett is one of the most successful investors in history. Born in 1930, he began investing as a teenager and built his company, Berkshire Hathaway, into one of the world's largest corporations.

Known as the "Oracle of Omaha," Buffett is famous for his long-term investment philosophy. He focuses on buying great businesses at fair prices and holding them for decades, rather than trading frequently.

His Investment Principles:

  • Understanding the businesses you invest in
  • Looking for companies with strong competitive advantages
  • Buying when prices are below the company's true value
  • Holding investments for the long term
  • Avoiding debt and focusing on cash generation

Buffett's approach has made him one of the wealthiest people in the world, and he's known for his simple lifestyle and commitment to giving away most of his fortune to charity.
