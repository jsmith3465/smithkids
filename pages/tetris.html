How to Play:

  • Arrow Keys: Move left/right, rotate, and drop pieces
  • Left/Right: Move piece horizontally
  • Up: Rotate piece
  • Down: Move piece down faster
  • Space: Drop piece instantly
  • P: Pause/Resume game
  • Complete horizontal lines to clear them and score points
  • Game speeds up as you level up!
