How to Play:

  • Arrow Keys: Move Pac-Man up, down, left, or right
  • Goal: Eat all the dots to complete the level
  • Power Pellets: Eat the large dots to turn ghosts blue and eat them for bonus points
  • Avoid Ghosts: Don't let the ghosts catch you!
  • P: Pause/Resume game
  • Complete levels to advance and increase your score!
